## Purpose

Governed artifacts must stay readable, reviewable, and stable under revision. "Terse style" is a writing constraint: short sections, explicit claims, and minimal rhetorical padding.

The goal is not aesthetics. The goal is reducing ambiguity and review cost.

Exact style rules and conformance expectations are defined in the normative edition.
