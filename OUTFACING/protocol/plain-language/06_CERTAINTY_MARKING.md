## Purpose

Certainty marking makes the status of claims visible:

- settled: treated as binding for a scope,
- provisional: tentative, task-specific, or awaiting review.

It also requires derivation marking for durable claims: stated, inferred, assumed (or equivalent), plus evidence pointers where required.

## Promotion (approval into durable state)

Update proposals are not durable state. They remain non-binding until explicit approval (ratification) into durable registries.

Storage is not approval.
