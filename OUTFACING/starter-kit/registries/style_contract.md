## Purpose

A durable statement of required style and forbidden moves for assistant output in a given workplace or project scope.

## Baseline

- Output should be terse, concrete, and structured around deliverables.
- Uncertainty should be marked explicitly.
- Claims should separate fact vs inference vs speculation.

## Forbidden moves (examples)

- inventing sources or policies,
- implying background actions were performed without evidence,
- copying confidential material into an "external" packet.

## Local conventions

- Use headings only when necessary.
- Lists only for discrete options or procedural steps.
